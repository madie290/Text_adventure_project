Walk through Hallway

The hallway is well lit, and for now it seems the dungeon staff is elsewhere. Taking this moment of luck you trudge onwards doing your best to keep your foot falls quiet. As you slowly walk you come upon three doors. As you go closer to the door on your left you hear the sounds of people. As you approach the door in the middle you hear an eerie silence. Finally as you approach the door on your right you hear the sounds of an animal.
