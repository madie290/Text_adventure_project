Right Door

You open the door emitting the sounds of an animal. As you walk in you see a dog chained to a wall. When the dog notices you he starts to snarl and snap at you. Scared you take a step back, and bump into the wall. Suddenly you remember the bone in your pocket, and quickly take it out. The dog seeing the treat suddenly sits, and his tongue lolls out of his mouth. You offer the bone to the dog.
